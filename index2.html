Why AJAC Exists

The existing toolkit - public relations, cybersecurity, and social monitoring - was built for a different era.

In the last decade, AI-powered manipulation, aggressive geopolitical actors, a collapse in trust, and coordinated cross-platform operations have created a threat landscape that no existing solution fully addresses.

At the same time, mis/disinformation is now the World Economic Forum’s #2 global short-term risk — above even economic shocks. Every business is exposed.

Who We Work With

We partner with all types and sizes of businesses to help them build resilience to disinformation. Financial institutions, energy providers, industry associations, and other high-stakes organizations exposed to geopolitical, social, or synthetic-media threats.

What We Do

Audit

Information risk audit to identify vulnerabilities to information manipulation and current capabilities.

Build

Create systems, triage and decision thresholds, map response pathways, and training.

Connect

Cross-functional integration of a network of communications, legal, cyber, security, policy, risk, and government experts.
