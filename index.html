Why AJAC Exists

Public relations, cybersecurity, and social monitoring were built for a different era. AI-industrialized false content, narrative-cyber convergence, collapsed trust, and coordinated cross-platform operations have created a threat no existing solution addresses.


Mis/disinformation is the World Economic Forum's #2 global short-term risk — above even economic shocks and climate events. Every major enterprise is exposed.

Who We Work With
Regulated sectors (e.g. financial and energy), reputation-sensitive firms (e.g. industry associations), and institutions exposed to geopolitical, social, or synthetic-media threats.
