Why AJAC Exists

The existing toolkit: public relations, cybersecurity, and social monitoring was built for a different era. In the last decade, AI-powered manipulation, aggressive geopolitical actors, a collapse in trust, and coordinated cross-platform operations have created a threat landscape that no existing solution adequately addresses.

Mis/disinformation is now the World Economic Forum’s #2 global short-term risk — above even economic shocks. Every business is exposed.

Who We Work With

We partner with financial institutions, energy providers, industry associations, and other high-stakes organizations exposed to geopolitical, social, or synthetic-media threats.

What We Do

Assess & Audit

A thorough information risk audit to understand vulnerabilities and current capabilities.

Build

Create systems, playbooks, training, and institutional memory for sustained resilience.

Connect

Cross-functional integration of C-suite, communications, legal, cyber, security, policy, risk, and government relations.

Respond

Implement triage and decision thresholds, map response pathways, and develop resilience planning.
